Primrose Center, Inc.’s Website Re-design

by Luis F. Calderon

The website redesign objective of Primrose Center is to re-organize, and create more interactive content, but most importantly is to create a long lasting relationship with visitors.

Site Intent

The intent for this project is to redesign Primrose Center Inc.’s website. I want to give the site a different feel to it by redesigning layout and structure of the site. Presently, the website is outdated and at first glance is difficult to determine what you can do because of the excess links and large sections. The website currently has 8 web pages. I plan to consolidate certain information to be able not only to shrink the website to 6 pages, but also make room for new material. Currently, Primrose Center’s website has the content distributed in the following pages: home, services, about us, affiliates, history, partners in giving, business partnerships and board of directors. My goal is to consolidate the following pages: affiliates,history, business partnerships and board of directors into the about us section. I feel all the content found in those pages all fall under the same category.

By consolidating these pages I will be able to make room for new content and keep the website at 6 webpages. The re-design of the site will keep the following pages: home, about us, and services. And the three new pages are: apply now, volunteer and testimonial. The purpose of creating these new pages is to increase website traffic, and engage visitors, so they want to become a member whether you want to apply for counseling or become a volunteer. Primrose Center is a non-profit organization so they need all the help they can get, and involving the community is key to success for organizations like Primrose Center. The other new page is testimonial, it actually will be the partners in giving page the website currently has, but with a different name.

By creating a user-friendly interface we can deliver a better experience to the visitors. Is important to keep a consistent pattern throughout the layout and organize content to direct potential members to the main call to action.

Competitive Analysis

After researching in Alexa.com, I found no data regarding Primrose Center’s website. Currently, this website has 0 traffic rank. When exploring the Primrose Center’s website, I feel the site needs more keywords to attract the visitors attention. This will definitely generate more traffic for the website by giving people a reason to stay on the site. Also, there are many non-profit organizations that thrive on community support. Primrose has a great opportunity in that area and it is to grab the attention of their community and close neighbors.

If Primrose Center wants to gain market share, they need to tap into their close by demographics. But, most important, the websites needs to have keywords that other competitors are using. Also, Primrose should practice on updating or creating new content for their users, that way the users don’t feel that they are in an obsolete site that has no type of new activity.

A competitor that I found in the Orlando area is Rockorlando.org. This organization is dedicated to transforming the lives of members of our society. Rock Orlando uses a biblical approach to change the live of individuals. The Rock Orlando website barely has no statistics. The only statistic that I found is that it has a traffic rank of 11,509,880 in global rank. The biggest strategy of the success of this website is the implementation of keywords and selection of images. Rockorlando.org has a great design, and can be an excellent reference when making design decisions for the re-design of Primrosecenter.org.
